About Me

Hi, my name is Harvey Reid. I grew up in Louisville, Kentucky, and have lived in Kentucky for most of my life. I went to college at Berea College in Kentucky, and obtained a Bachelor's degree in English with a writing concentration.

I lived there for four years and graduated in 2018. I have recently decided to go back to school and I am currently pursuing an Associate's degree in Programming at Jefferson Community Technical College.

I currently work a warehouse job in the morning, and come home in the afternoon to work on my online classes. I am scheduled to be finished with my degree after the upcoming Fall semester, and I am currently seeking a programming internship for the fall.

In my free time I love to go running, play video games, and spend time with family. My family includes my parents, two sisters, and my little niece. My parents have a dog named Bacca, and my sister has a dog named Momo.
